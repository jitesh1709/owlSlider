About Us


Conceptualised in 2010 “Rita’s Art Studio” is a fine art studio in the heart of guwahati city run by Rita Chhawchharia.The studio offers state of art quality work in traditional tanjore paintings as well as contemparary modern art in oils and acrylics. Also various hobby classes for tanjores, oils, acrylics, mixed media as well as variety of DIY courses are conducted here. Apart from learning art this is also the place to destress through art. Its “the place” for anyone who wants to experience the art inside themselves, in a silent, encouraging safe place.


Visual Art Forms



Oil paintings


This is the process of painting with pigments with a medium of drying oil as the binder.Since after 15th century, oil paints eventually became the principal medium used to create artworks as its advantages became widely known.

Tanjore


This is a classical South Indian Painting style characterised by rich ,flat ,vivid colours .Mostly they are iconic compositions with glittering gold foils overlaid on delicate but extensive gesso work and inlay of precious and semi precious stones.

Mix Media


This is an artwork in which more than one medium or material has been employed.There is no fixed rule applicable for this kind of artwork.Artist may use cloth,paper,wood,clay,etc. to create a single piece of art.
